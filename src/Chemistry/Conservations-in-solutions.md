### Algorithm 1

If \\( c(A^+) + c(B^+) = c(C^-) + c(D^-) \\)

\\( c(A^+) > c(B^-) > c(C^+) > c(D^-) \\) and \\( c(A^+) > c(C^+) > c(B^-) > c(D^-) \\) are false

In other word, The first and last ion must be both positive or both negative.
